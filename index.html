Qeema Swap

The app idea in one sentence

Qeema Swap turns unused items into exchange value, while the platform controls delivery and safety.

Positioning

Trade-only marketplace

Users do not sell items and do not add cash. They only trade item vs item through structured offers.

Trust angle

No direct meetups

The app removes the biggest friction point in peer-to-peer marketplaces: arranging safe physical meetings.

Revenue angle

Delivery-led income

Since every trade requires your delivery service, delivery fees and protection fees become core monetization.

Updated business rules

These rules reflect your requested changes: no selling, no cash, delivery only from your side.

Allowed

  • Item-for-item trade only.
  • Users can post items they no longer want.
  • Users can suggest what they want in exchange.
  • Users can offer one item or a bundle of multiple items.
  • Your team handles pickup, item check, and delivery.
  • Users can pay delivery, protection, verification, or listing visibility fees.

Not allowed

  • No selling option.
  • No cash top-up between users.
  • No “cash-only” offer.
  • No user-to-user meeting option.
  • No exchanging restricted or unsafe categories.
  • No off-app contact sharing inside chat.
Key strategic implication: removing cash makes the app safer and cleaner, but it also makes matching harder. To solve this, the MVP should support bundles, wishlists, match score, and flexible “acceptable categories.”

How the trade should work

A controlled end-to-end flow makes the app easier to trust and easier to monetize.

Post item

User uploads photos, brand, condition, estimated value, and wanted item categories.

Receive offers

Other users offer items from their own listed inventory. No cash offers are allowed.

Accept trade

Both users confirm the exchange and pay platform delivery or protection fees.

Pickup & check

Your delivery team collects both items and performs basic visual condition checks.

Deliver & rate

Items are delivered to both sides. Users confirm receipt and rate each other.

Exclusive delivery model

This is the biggest differentiator. It improves safety, but it also increases operational responsibility.

Recommended at launch

Start in Amman only

Launch inside Amman first to control delivery costs, reduce failed pickups, and learn the operational flow.

Operational need

Two-sided pickup

Every trade needs pickup from both users, basic check, and delivery to both users. This makes routing important.

Trust feature

Inspection checkpoint

Delivery staff should upload item photos and condition notes before delivery is completed.

Pros, cons, and risks

The trade-only + controlled delivery model is safer, but it needs strong operations.

Pros
  • Clear differentiation from OpenSooq, dubizzle, and Facebook Marketplace.
  • Safer user experience because users never meet each other.
  • Delivery becomes a natural revenue stream.
  • Item-only trade can support sustainability and reuse positioning.
  • Platform has stronger control over completed trades and ratings.
Cons / risks
  • Matching is harder without cash top-ups.
  • Delivery operations can become costly if routes are inefficient.
  • Disputes may increase if item condition is not checked well.
  • Low-value items may not justify delivery cost.
  • Restricted/prohibited items need strong moderation.

Recommended roadmap

A phased launch reduces cost and keeps operations under control.

Phase 1

Prototype + validation

Landing page, brand identity, clickable app prototype, 30–50 user interviews, and category testing.

Phase 2

Amman MVP

Launch with 5–7 categories, controlled delivery, simple trade flow, and manual admin support.

Phase 3

Scale

Add stronger verification, optimized delivery routes, subscriptions, category sponsors, and more cities.

Best MVP success metric: not downloads. Track successful completed trades, repeat traders, average delivery margin, dispute rate, and offer-to-trade conversion.

الملخص بالعربي

التطبيق المقترح هو منصة أردنية للتبادل فقط، بحيث يعرض المستخدم غرضاً لا يحتاجه، ويستقبل عروضاً من مستخدمين آخرين بغرض مقابل غرض. لا يوجد بيع، ولا دفع نقدي بين المستخدمين، ولا لقاء مباشر بينهم. التوصيل والاستلام يكون حصرياً من طرفكم، وهذا يعطي التطبيق ميزة أمان وثقة ويخلق مصدر دخل واضح من رسوم التوصيل والحماية والاشتراكات والإعلانات المدروسة.

أنسب إطلاق أولي يكون داخل عمّان فقط، وبفئات محددة مثل الموبايلات والإكسسوارات، الألعاب، مستلزمات الأطفال، الكتب، والأغراض المنزلية الصغيرة. أهم مؤشرات النجاح هي عدد التبادلات المكتملة، نسبة تكرار المستخدمين، هامش ربح التوصيل، ونسبة الشكاوى.
